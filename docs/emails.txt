I hope you are doing good! Yes, a simple tangent space + logistic regression pipeline worked better than chance for rhythmic repetition of imagery. It's fun because I spent a long time trying to classify the whole repetition signal as one trial, expecting any signal enhancement, but only when I managed each repetition as a single trial did I get better results (also many more trials). I am attaching a .py file with the code. In essence, I filtered the signal into 20 bands, converted the trials to cov matrices, projected them with the TS function, concatenated everything, and let the logistic regressor weight the features.
